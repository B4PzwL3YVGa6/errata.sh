errata.sh

errata.sh is a shell script that implements a comprehensive suite of documentation-related tests. It's designed to be a drop-in solution to your quality assurance needs.

  • Self-contained

    errata.sh only depends on Node.js, which is installed by the script.

  • CI/CD Ready

    errata.sh is compatible with all major CI services, including Travis CI, CircleCI, and Jenkins.

  • Open Source

    errata.sh consists entirely of MIT-licensed, open-source components.

Comprehensive Tests
  • 1
    Markup & Front Matter
    Ensure your markup and front matter are valid and well-structured by leveraging the remark-lint and gray-matter libraries.
  • 2
    Style & Spelling
    Use vale to identify spelling and style mistakes according to your own style guide.
  • 3
    Code Examples
    Manage your code snippets outside of your markup with blocktest—allowing you to maintain thorough test suites for your examples.
  • 4
    Accessibility
    Keep your generated HTML accessible with the a11y linter powered by the Chrome Accessibility Tools.
About
